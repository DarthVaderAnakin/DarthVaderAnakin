Welcome to Space



Supernovae, colossal stellar explosions, can outshine entire galaxies for a brief period. They come in different types and play a crucial role in synthesizing heavy elements like gold and silver. Their shockwaves trigger the formation of new stars and influence galaxy evolution. Supernova remnants persist for thousands of years, serving as cosmic laboratories. They've also historically shaped our understanding of the universe.
